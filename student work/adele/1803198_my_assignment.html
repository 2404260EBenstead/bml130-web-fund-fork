Jaxon

My cute Cockapoo puppy

Jaxon is four months old. He has almost doubled in size since we got him in August. He's the friendliest dog I have ever met and he loves people, dogs, and pretty much everything he sees! He will take any opportunity he can to meet new people and gets particularly excited when the postman/workmen/or anyone else knocks on the door. He will run straight out and greet them. He has recently discovered the wonder of people food....it did not agree with him!
