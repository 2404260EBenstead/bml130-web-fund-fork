Anyone interested in joining the club, should either email us on Stirlandscricketclub@yahoo.com or contact our Chairman Richard Gubbins

A Clubmark accredited club in West Sussex who always looking to welcome new members of all ages and ability.

We currently field 2 mens teams in the Sussex League, a friendly side on Sundays & several junior sides, ranging from U18s through to U10s, as well as various indoor sides over the winter.
